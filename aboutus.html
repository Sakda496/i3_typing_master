Who We Are

We are a team of developers passionate about improving typing speed and accuracy for learners and professionals alike. Our Typing Test App is designed to help users improve their skills in a fun and interactive way.

This project started as a simple tool to practice typing and has now grown into a full-featured platform where you can track your progress, challenge yourself, and become a faster, more confident typist.
